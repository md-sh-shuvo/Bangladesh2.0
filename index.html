The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to
enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh is not just a country with challenges; it is a nation of possibilities. We have shown the world that even with limited resources, creativity, courage, and a vision for change can transform lives. Through microfinance and social businesses, we turned a simple idea into a global movement, proving that poverty is not a lack of money but a lack of opportunity. Bangladesh stands as a beacon of hope, a testament to the power of innovation and the resilience of its people to rise above adversity and build a future filled with dignity and prosperity."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
